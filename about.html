    I like the idea of how we are all like stars. We form starclusters, social bubbles, then the solar systme. Some find their orbits while others still try to figure out how to navigate in this world.

    With this metaphor in my heart, lonely cosmos is a way to properly acknowlege all the changes coming with the graduation and me figuring out how to face the uncertainty of future and beyond.
